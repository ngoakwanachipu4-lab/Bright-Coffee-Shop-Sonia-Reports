# Bright-Coffee-Shop-Sonia-Reports
--PROBLEM--
The coffee shop 's new CEO wanted to h insights and recommendations  to grow the company’s revenue and improve product performance.

--ACTION--
SQL ETL was used to analyze the current performace of the shop.
--RESULTS--

--TOOLS--
the tools that were used include Miro , Excel, SQL,Canva and power point.

the insights are as follows:

Low  sales of product categories have been recorded in January and February due to the fact that it is summer and temperatures are high.

Sales started to increase (at 20% to 30%) from March to June because temperatures are dropping, winter is starting.

High sales are recorded from Monday to Friday of the week, while weekends have recorded low sales.

Peaks are between 06;00am and 10:00am, while low sales are recorded from 11:00am to 20:00pm, this could be that people prefer coffee when they start their day.

Lower Manhattan and Astria have recorded low sales as compared to Hell’s Kitchen.

Products categories that recorded high sales include coffee, tea, drinking chocolate and bakery. All other product categories have recorded low sales.

Product categories including coffee, tea, drinking chocolate and bakery have recorded high sales during weekdays than weekend, probably because customers are not working on those days.
